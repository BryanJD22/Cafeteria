Points of interest of the agreement :


  • The workers affected by this agreement shall enjoy thirty-one calendar days of vacation per year of service.

  • The workers will enjoy between the months of June to September of, at least, fifteen uninterrupted calendar days of their vacation period or the proportional part that corresponds to them according to the date of entry in the company.

  • The workers of the company will have priority to fill the vacancies that occur, provided that they meet the suitable requirements for it.

  • The principle of equality in the workplace will be respected for all purposes, not admitting discrimination for reasons of age, sex, ideology, religion, race, disability, etc.

  • Companies shall respect the right of all workers to freely join unions and shall not discriminate or make the employment of a worker dependent on the condition that he/she does not join or renounces his/her union membership.
